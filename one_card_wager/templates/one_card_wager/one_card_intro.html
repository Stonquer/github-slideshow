One Card Wager Rules:

One Card Wager is a simple poker-style game. After adding a compulsory ante, one card from a randomized deck is dealt to each player. The first player to act can check or bet. The next player then responds by either checking or betting if checked to, or calling a bet or folding when a previous player bets.

Once all players have acted and there are no uncalled bets, the pot is awarded to the player with the highest-ranking card. In the event of a tie, the pot is split equally.
